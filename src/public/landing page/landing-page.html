Time tracking is mandatory since May 12 2019

Since the release of the RD- Ley 8/2019, companies hae been required to record the workday of each worker. This day log will be daily and must include the time of entry and exit, without prejudice to the flexibility of time. Employees must be aware of the duration and distribution of their ordinary work day, as well as their work schedule.

“The section 4 c), Art. 12, establishes with respect to part-time workers: “For these purposes, the part-time workers' day will be recorded day by day and will be totaled monthly, delivering a copy to the worker, together with the receipt of salaries and of the summary of all the hours made in each month, both ordinary and complementary ”.”

Boletín Oficial del Estado, BOE
